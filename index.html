THE ALCHEMY OF SILVER

Sterling silver is the brand's favourite material. It has indeed a remarkable property, its great malleability which makes possible to conceive audacious jewels. All the bergue’s jewelry is made of 925 sterling silver.

The elegance of design jewelry

When we seizes the precious metal, silver, it subtly shakes up conventions. He can be scratched, brushed, blackened, bleached, frosted, printed and thus acquires a unique patina. Bergue jewellery becomes the unnecessary indispensable that reveal your personality.

THE LUXURY OF HANDMADE

The brand claims to reconciliate art and craftsmanship and believes in the intelligence of the hand. At Bergue we create by making. All the jewels are created and manufactured by hand in the brand’studio-workshop.
